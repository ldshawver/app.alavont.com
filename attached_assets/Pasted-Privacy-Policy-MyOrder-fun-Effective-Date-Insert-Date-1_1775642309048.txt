1. Introduction

MyOrder.fun (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is built with a privacy-first and security-first architecture. Unlike traditional platforms, we intentionally minimize data storage and retain only what is strictly necessary for real-time operation.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and handle your information while prioritizing data minimization, limited retention, and system security.

By using MyOrder.fun, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Core Privacy Principles

MyOrder.fun operates under the following principles:

Minimal Data Collection – Only essential data is collected
Ephemeral Data Storage – Data is deleted as soon as it is no longer required
User-Controlled Deletion – Users can remove their data permanently
Security-First Design – All systems are designed to reduce attack surfaces
No Data Monetization – We do not sell, rent, or exploit user data

5. Ephemeral Data & Deletion Policy
5.1 Order Data Deletion
Order history is not permanently stored
All order-related data is deleted immediately after successful payment processing

Data is not retained for future recovery
